About

Discover and acquire in one place: your Wishlist (books you want but don't own yet), plus Upcoming / New releases from authors you follow (Google Books + OpenLibrary), and Libby library search. Owned books live on the Library page. Word counts are estimates — Calibre provides real counts once a book is owned.

Maintenance

Re-run the title cleanup / junk filters / dedup + OpenLibrary cross-reference over the already-cached releases — drops stale placeholders and re-matches ownership. No Google quota (unlike Check now, which fetches fresh releases). Rarely needed day to day.
